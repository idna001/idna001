### 👨‍💻 About Me

I'm a self-taught developer focused on building clean, modern web & app experiences.

- 🎨 Building UIs with **React**, **Tailwind CSS** & **DaisyUI** — keeping things clean and component-driven
- 📱 Currently learning **React Native** for mobile development
- 🖥️ Running a homeserver on **Proxmox** with Home Assistant, Frigate & various self-hosted services on the side
- 📸 Fun fact — I love photography and riding my motorcycle 🏍️
